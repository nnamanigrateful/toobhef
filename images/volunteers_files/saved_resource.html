Our Aims and Objectives.

Our Seven main aims and objectives are explaining our general purpose in ensuring that the poor, the needy and the less privileged are getting the overall best attention and care that is deserving for every human being.

  • To give Free General Health Education to all especially the Poor Masses and the Less Privileged.
  • To offer Free Medical Screening Tests e.g.; HIV, Hepatitis, Malaria, Diabetes, Eye Problems, and More to the entire population especially the poor and the vulnerable groups.
  • To dispense Free Drugs to the deserving individuals.
  • To offer Free Health Counselling to the Needy, General and Vulnerable Population.
  • To Direct and Facilitate referral to the Appropriate Specialists where needed.
  • To offer Consultancy Services in Education especially to the needy and less privileged.
  • To fill the Gap between the Less Privileged and access to good health services.
  • Awarding Scholarships to Deserving and Needy Students.
