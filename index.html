    About Us

    Discover Our Inspiring

    Mission Today

    Welcome to Theresa Onuego Mega Brain Health and Education Foundation (TOOBEF), a beacon of hope and transformation in Enugu, Nigeria. Our foundation, spearheaded by the distinguished neurosurgeon and educator, Dr. Ozor I. I., is committed to making a profound impact on the lives of the less privileged.

    Our Mission

    At TOOBEF, our mission is clear: to empower and uplift the less privileged members of our society. We believe in the transformative power of accessible healthcare, quality education, and humanitarian support. Through these pillars, we aim to create a brighter future for those who need it most.

    Our Programs

    Medical Awareness

    We conduct targeted health education programs to create awareness about crucial medical issues within our community.

    Medical Consultation

    We provide free medical consultation and treatments, ensuring that even the most vulnerable have access to healthcare services.

    Humanitarian Aid

    Our foundation distributes essential items, including food, toiletries, and other hospitality items, to those in need, offering comfort and relief during challenging times.

    Scholarships

    TOOBEF extends a helping hand to promising students by offering scholarships at the primary, secondary, and tertiary levels, empowering them to pursue their dreams and aspirations.
