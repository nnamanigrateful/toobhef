    About Us

    Discover Our Inspiring

    Mission Today

    Welcome to Theresa Onuego Mega Brain Health and Education Foundation (TOOBHEF), a beacon of hope and transformation in Enugu, Nigeria. Our foundation, spearheaded by the distinguished neurosurgeon and educator, Dr. Ozor I. I., is committed to making a profound impact on the lives of the less privileged.

    Our Mission

    At TOOBHEF, our mission is clear: to empower and uplift the less privileged members of our society. We believe in the transformative power of accessible healthcare, quality education, and humanitarian support. Through these pillars, we aim to create a brighter future for those who need it most.

    Our Programs

    Medical Awareness

    We conduct targeted health education programs to create awareness about crucial medical issues within our community.

    Medical Check-Up

    We provide free medical consultation and treatments, ensuring that even the most vulnerable have access to healthcare services.

    Humanitarian Aid

    Our foundation distributes essential items, including food, toiletries, and other hospitality items, to those in need, offering comfort and relief during challenging times.

    Scholarships

    TOOBHEF extends a helping hand to promising students by offering scholarships at the primary, secondary, and tertiary levels, empowering them to pursue their dreams and aspirations.

    Asked Questions

    TOOBHEF stands for the "Theresa Onuego Mega Brain Health and Education Foundation." We are a non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to improving the lives of the less privileged through healthcare, education, and humanitarian initiatives

    TOOBHEF was founded by Dr. Ozor I. I., a distinguished neurosurgeon and educator. Dr. Ozor brings expertise in the field of neurosurgery and a deep commitment to community service.

    Our mission is to empower individuals and communities by providing accessible healthcare, educational opportunities, and support to those in need, especially the less privileged.

    TOOBHEF offers several programs, including:

    • Medical Awareness: Educational initiatives to raise awareness about health-related issues.
    • Free Medical Screening and Treatment: Providing free medical consultations and treatment to those who cannot afford healthcare.
    • Humanitarian Aid: Distributing essential items like food, toiletries, and hospitality goods.
    • Scholarships: Offering educational scholarships at primary, secondary, and tertiary levels.

    You can get involved in several ways:

    • Make a donation to support our programs.
    • Volunteer your time and expertise.
    • Attend our events and fundraisers.
    • Spread the word about TOOBHEF on social media and among your network.
